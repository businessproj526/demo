How It Works

  • Expansionary Monetary Policy: Lowers interest rates, increases money supply, boosts investments and jobs, but may slightly weaken BoP.
  • Expansionary Fiscal Policy: Lowers taxes and increases government spending, boosts consumption and jobs, but increases imports and weakens BoP.
  • Contractionary Monetary Policy: Raises interest rates, reduces borrowing and investment, decreases jobs, but may improve BoP.
  • Contractionary Fiscal Policy: Raises taxes and cuts government spending, reduces consumption and jobs, but decreases imports and improves BoP.

Goal: Try to get both Jobs and BoP into the green zone (70-100%)!

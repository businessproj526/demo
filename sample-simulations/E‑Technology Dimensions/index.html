📚 Agricultural Best Practices

🌱 Seed Treatment: Treat seeds with fungicide before sowing to prevent soil-borne diseases and improve germination rates.
💧 Water Management: Use drip irrigation to save 30-50% water while maintaining optimal soil moisture levels.
🧪 Soil Testing: Conduct soil tests every 2-3 years to determine nutrient requirements and pH levels for optimal crop growth.
🐛 Integrated Pest Management: Combine biological, cultural, and chemical methods to control pests sustainably.
